About Me

Peter Hwang is an Korean-American male born in Binghampton, New York.

If 3 words were used to define his character and personality, it would be competitive, curious, and charisma. Yes... the 3 C's...

Peter has traveled to 12 different countries and has lived in 8 different states in America. He is fluent in both Korean and English. But he can also say about 5 important sentences in 4 other languages.

Peter is also a musician. He had a music album out in his early 20's as an r&b artist. Now he works at a music publishing company located in New York as head of the A&R department.
